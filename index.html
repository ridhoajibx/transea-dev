OUR SERVICES

SHIPPING AGENCY

TRANSEA GLOBAL INDO provides the best agency service for regular and tramping ships that calling Batam Port and other Indonesia Ports.

Our professional staffs are working 24-hour a day to take care of any requirements or problems with which our clients may be encountered, including:

  • Port Master, Customs, Sanitary, and Immigration Clearance
  • Cargo Operation: Stevedoring
  • Bunkering and Fresh Water
  • Supply and Delivery of Provisions
  • Crew Changes & Welfare
  • Garbage & Sludge removal

CHARTERING & BROKERING

PT. TRANSEA GLOBAL INDO has acknowledged the importance the role of chartering and brokering business in shipping. We therefore have been working and developing many cargo and ship fixtures with many reliable shipowners, ship operators, shippers, consignees, other brokers and charterers on a worldwide basis.

PT. TRANSEA GLOBAL INDO has wide ranging experience of working closely with clients to understand their requirements. We then make sure that in every step of the negotiations, the signing of the fixtures, and the carrying-out of the agreements will be focused on the suitable cargo, the right vessel, competitive freight, efficient hire, and effective timing.

PT. TRANSEA GLOBAL INDO dedicates its Chartering and Brokering Department to provide the services such as chartering and brokering all type of ships, including convensional cargo ships, tugs and barges, landing crafts, offshore-support vessels based on voyage charter, time charter, and bareboat charter. Related to those services, we also handle sale and purchases all type of vessels.

PROJECT LOGISTICS

PT. TRANSEA GLOBAL INDO offers comprehensive services in logistics services for breakbulk and project cargo to deliver your cargo to any destination around the globe. We will make sure that your oversize or overweight cargo will be safe in our hands because our key focus is on safe, efficient, cost effective and timely service solutions in moving the cargo.

With the privilege of good relationship with the shipping lines, airlines and reliable global networks, we are able to offer attractive rates to our customers for following services:

  • Door to Door Services
  • Import, Export and Customs Clearance
  • Air Freight Service
  • Sea Freight Service
  • Inland Freight Service
